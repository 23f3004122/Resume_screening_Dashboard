Salesforce Engineer - Job Description

We are seeking a Salesforce Engineer with strong experience in Apex, Lightning (LWC), integrations, and platform customization. The ideal candidate will design and implement scalable solutions on the Salesforce platform, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and ensure best practices in development, testing, and deployment.

Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and maintain custom solutions using Apex, Visualforce, and Lightning Web Components (LWC).
- Build and optimize integrations using REST/SOAP APIs, OAuth, and middleware (e.g., MuleSoft).
- Implement robust data models, sharing/security models, and automation (Flows, Process Builder).
- Write unit tests and maintain high code coverage with test classes.
- Configure and manage Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, and Experience Cloud features.
- Collaborate with Admins, PMs, and business stakeholders to refine requirements and deliver features iteratively.
- Use SFDX, Git-based workflows, and CI/CD tools for reliable deployments.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of hands-on Salesforce development experience.
- Proficiency in Apex, SOQL, SOSL, and Lightning (LWC).
- Experience with APIs, integrations, and OAuth.
- Strong understanding of Salesforce security/sharing, profiles, permission sets.
- Familiarity with SFDX, scratch orgs, DevOps practices, and unit testing.
- Salesforce certifications (e.g., Platform Developer I/II, Admin) are a plus.
